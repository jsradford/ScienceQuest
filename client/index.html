What is Science Quest?

Science Quest! is a free massively open online playing game for science built from Mozilla's BrowserQuest. Our mission is to enable people all over the world to participate in the latest behavioral scientific studies in a fun, fantastical way. The studies in ScienceQuest are all actual research carried out by scientists on Volunteer Science.

WHY PLAY? Researchers need participants from across the globe. We want to make it fun, easy, and meaningful for you to participate.

HOW TO PLAY? Point and click to move. Talk to characters to participate in their studies and earn new weapons and armor to fight the monsters.
